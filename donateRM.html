Help Us, Feed Them

Helping Hands, Full Plates

Every day, tons of edible food is discarded while thousands go hungry. At RescueMark, we not only connect surplus food to budget-conscious buyers — we also partner with local NGOs and charity organizations to deliver food to those who need it most.

What most consider "waste" can become someone's next meal. This batch of near-expiry but perfectly edible items was rescued from local supermarkets and redirected to a Johannesburg shelter. In just one afternoon, volunteers sorted and delivered over 200 food parcels — saving lives and reducing landfill waste in one act of kindness.

Through our partnership with local NGOs, these recovered food donations reached communities hit hardest by food insecurity. Many of the recipients shared how they'd gone days without a proper meal. What would’ve been discarded ended up on dinner plates — a powerful reminder that good food should never go to waste.

We rarely think about the value of food that’s close to expiry — but to these children, it meant lunchboxes full of nourishment. Our network ensures that rescued food meets safety standards and reaches the most vulnerable in time. Smiles like these are what your donations make possible.
